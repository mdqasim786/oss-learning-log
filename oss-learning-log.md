## Lesson 2 — How Maintainers Think

### What I learned

- A maintainer is responsible for the long-term health of a project.
- Their goal is to keep the project stable and maintainable, not simply accept more code.
- Small, focused pull requests are easier to review and are more likely to be merged.
- Every contribution should reduce the maintainer's workload rather than create more work.

### Key takeaway

I should think like a maintainer before acting like a contributor.
